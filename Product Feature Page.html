Features

Design creation and Upload

Stylique features a built-in editor that allows users to create custom t-shirt designs using tools for shapes, text, and images. Users can also upload their own graphics, enabling diverse artistic expressions. Completed designs can be submitted for public viewing and interaction, fostering a vibrant creative community.

Ranking board

The Ranking Board displays newly uploaded t-shirt designs for two weeks, allowing users to vote on their favorites. Designs are ranked by votes, creating a competitive environment that rewards creativity and increases visibility for top designs.

User interaction

Users engage with designs through a simple voting system, allowing each user to cast one vote for their favorite. Additionally, users can pledge a monetary amount with their vote, indicating their intent to purchase the t-shirt if the design wins, fostering community support for designers.
